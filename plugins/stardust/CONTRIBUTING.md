# Contributing

**Learning-corpus contributions** (captures, critiques, signoffs that
grow stardust's moves catalog and exemplar corpus):

> Open Claude in this repo and say *"I am a contributor"*. Stardust
> will identify you, look at what you've done before, and walk you
> through whatever makes sense in the moment. Phrasing isn't
> load-bearing.

**Code contributions** (sub-skill changes, runtime behavior, schema
edits):

> Open a PR with the change and one paragraph explaining what shifted.
> The maintainer team reviews against the schemas and the runtime
> contract in `skills/stardust/reference/`.

That's it. Schemas, worked examples, and the FAQ live in the
contributor-session experience and the reference docs — not duplicated
here.
