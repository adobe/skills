# List of workspace templates

Source: https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/workfront/using/adobe-workfront-planning/adobe-workfront-planning-architecture/workspace-templates
Last update: Thu May 01 2025

WFP ships with templates organized in two sections: Operational Record Types and Taxonomies.

## Basic: Marketing Management
Foundational marketing system.
- Operational: Campaign (15 fields)
- Taxonomies: Personas (7), Brands (6), Products (5)

## Advanced: Marketing Management
More nuanced marketing strategy.
- Operational: Campaigns (14), Programs (23)
- Taxonomies: Regions (1), Customer Journey Stages (4), Audiences (8), Deliverables (12), Brands (6)

## Enterprise: Marketing Management
Large/mature orgs with complex systems.
- Operational: Campaigns (15), Programs (15), Tactics (11), Activities (18)
- Taxonomies: Products (10), Deliverables (18), Target Audiences (8), Regions (8), Sub-regions (7), Partners (7), Use Cases (7), Customer Journey Stages (3)

## Sales management
- Operational: Opportunity (11), Activity (9), Campaign (8)
- Taxonomies: Account (7), Lead (14), Contact (12), Region (3), Industry (2), Buying Center (1), Product/Service (1), Competition (1)

## Product management
- Operational: Theme (10), Initiative (10), Epic (12), User Story (11)
- Taxonomies: Customer (8), Sprint (9), Product Team (4), Feature Requests (9), Industry (2)
